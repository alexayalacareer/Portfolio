Automated Pedestrian Crosswalk Warning System

Designed a sensor-driven warning concept focused on visibility, reliability, and clear driver signaling under real conditions.

Overview

This project explored an automated crosswalk warning concept that detects pedestrian presence and provides clear, attention-grabbing driver alerts. The design focus was on practical sensing, reliability in outdoor conditions, and simple signaling that improves visibility without requiring complex user interaction.

Tools

  • Concept development and system architecture
  • Sensor selection and detection logic planning
  • Signal design and human factors thinking
  • Reliability and safety considerations

Highlights

  • Designed a sensor-triggered warning flow for drivers
  • Focused on visibility, clarity, and outdoor reliability
  • Considered false triggers and safe failure behavior
  • Emphasized simple, intuitive signaling under real conditions
