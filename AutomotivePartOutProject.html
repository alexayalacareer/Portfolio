Overview

This project involved parting out and selling components from a 2002 Mazda MX-5 Miata LS after a motor failure, turning a non-functional vehicle into a learning experience in mechanical disassembly, systems understanding, and value recovery. The goal was to maximize return on investment by identifying, removing, and selling usable components while gaining hands-on mechanical experience.

Objectives & Challenges

The primary objective was to develop practical automotive skills while recovering value from a broken vehicle. Major challenges included inventorying and organizing dozens of components, determining which assemblies to keep intact, and managing sales within a limited timeframe before relocation. Mechanical challenges included dealing with rusted bolts, tight clearances, and complex fastener systems ‚Äî all of which required patience, creativity, and problem-solving in real time.
