Automotive Part Out Project

Organized a teardown, cataloging, and resale workflow with focus on process discipline, documentation, and an ROI mindset.

Overview

This project focused on building a structured part-out workflow from start to finish: vehicle teardown, component identification, condition assessment, cataloging, and resale preparation. The emphasis was on repeatable process, clean documentation, and practical decision making driven by time, value, and return.

Tools

  • Teardown planning and organization
  • Part labeling and photo documentation
  • Inventory tracking (spreadsheets)
  • Basic pricing and ROI evaluation

Highlights

  • Built a repeatable workflow for cataloging and resale preparation
  • Developed consistent documentation habits for parts and condition
  • Applied an ROI mindset to prioritize high value components
  • Improved organization and process discipline over time
